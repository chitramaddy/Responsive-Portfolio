About Me

I started my career in client relations with a touch of sales/marketing here and there. After 5 years in client relations, moved to the US of A. Then priorities changed, visa status changed and so did life. The time I spent away from career got me thinking. Client relations is all fine, but I wanted much more.

A career in which I can nurture my creativity; I can use my critical thinking. This combined with my awe for the world wide web lead me to the Rutgers Bootcamp on Web Developement. It has been a fascinating journey since then.

Did I say fascinating but hectic? There is no free time, whatsoever. If I get any freetime, I would love to relax with a cup of coffee and a book.
